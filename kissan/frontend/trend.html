Understanding Price Trends

Seasonal Patterns

Crop prices often follow seasonal cycles based on planting and harvest periods, weather conditions, and market demand.

Supply & Demand

Prices fluctuate based on the balance between available supply and consumer demand in local and global markets.

Weather Impact

Extreme weather events can significantly affect crop yields, leading to price volatility in agricultural markets.
